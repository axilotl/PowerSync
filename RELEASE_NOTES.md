## What's Changed

**Hide dashboard sections from customize mode**
The generated Home Assistant dashboard now lets users hide cards directly from Customize layout mode. Hidden sections are remembered in the browser, stay out of saved layouts, and can be restored with Show hidden or Reset layout, making it easier to remove redundant price or TOU cards for tariffs that do not need them.

**Improve the Battery Health dashboard card**
Battery Health now renders as a dedicated PowerSync dashboard card instead of a cramped grid of small tiles and a markdown footer. The new card shows the overall health score, capacity ratio, source and scan metadata, and per-pack rows with full labels and progress bars so Powerwall 3 and expansion-pack systems are easier to read.
