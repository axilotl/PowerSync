## What's Changed

**Amber Site Selection in Options Flow**
The Amber settings page (Settings → Devices & Services → PowerSync → Configure → Pricing → Amber) now shows a site dropdown when your Amber account has multiple NMIs. Previously the form only offered a token update field and immediately saved — there was no way to change which site PowerSync was using without going through a full re-setup. The dropdown is pre-populated by fetching your current sites at form load time, and the selection is saved to the integration config on submit.
