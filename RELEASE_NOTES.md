## What's Changed

**Hide inferred non-Tesla energy split cards**
PowerSync no longer invents Tesla-style source and destination split fields for non-Tesla systems that only provide aggregate daily energy totals. The mobile app will keep showing reliable generation, import, export, home, charge, and discharge totals, while hiding unsupported split cards such as Solar to Home/Battery/Grid and Battery From Solar/Grid instead of showing guessed breakdowns.
