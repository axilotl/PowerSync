## What's Changed

**Flow Power account pricing now uses account TWAP**
PowerSync now uses the Flow Power account/import TWAP from KWatch or portal account data when calculating Flow Power PEA pricing. This brings the dashboard, optimiser, and inverter tariff uploads closer to Flow Power Live Data for accounts where the account TWAP differs materially from the local rolling wholesale TWAP.
